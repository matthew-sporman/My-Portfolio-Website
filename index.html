My Story

Hello! I’m a Computer Science major at Michigan Technological University with a minor in Mathematical Sciences.

My passion for technology and problem-solving has guided me through hands-on experiences in both academic projects and professional environments. Currently, I work at Michigan Tech I.T., where I manage university-owned equipment, provide technical support, and train new employees. My experience spans a range of operating systems, including Windows, MacOS, and Linux distributions.

I’ve also worked on a variety of development projects, including creating a full-stack shopping portal and improving the Michigan Tech student portal interface. These experiences have helped me grow my skills in web development, software development life cycle, and version control. Outside of tech, I’ve led the Grand Blanc Marching Band’s trumpet section, where I mentored fellow musicians and helped organize rehearsals.

I'm always looking for new challenges to apply my skills and make a meaningful impact through technology!
